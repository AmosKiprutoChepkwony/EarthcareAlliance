Our Mission

To empower communities and organizations to protect and conserve wetland ecosystems, combat climate change, and promote environmental sustainability through strategic solutions, education, and innovation.

Our Vision

We envision a world where wetlands are thriving, communities are resilient, and the environment is preserved for future generations through inclusive, sustainable practices and innovative climate action.

Our Story

EarthCare Alliance was founded with a passion for protecting Kenya's unique ecosystems and addressing the urgent challenges posed by climate change. Our journey began with a dedicated team of environmentalists and community leaders who recognized the power of collaborative solutions. Since then, we have grown into a trusted consultancy offering innovative strategies and practical solutions for climate resilience and ecosystem restoration. By focusing on wetland conservation and empowering local communities, we are building a future that is both sustainable and inclusive.
